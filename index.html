Research highlights

Higgs self‑coupling (HH, HHH)

Searches for HH and HHH with b‑jets and τ leptons; sensitivity and reconstruction studies spanning resolved, semi‑boosted, and boosted topologies.

Jet‑charge tagging

Developing charge‑aware heavy‑flavor tagging to lift combinatorics and strengthen measurements and searches in heavy‑flavor final states.

Particle reconstruction with transformers

Generalized SPA‑Net for simultaneous boosted/resolved reconstruction and unambiguous interpretation across topologies.

Trigger strategies for rare signals

Parking and b‑tag triggers enabling access to previously unreachable phase space in Run‑3 and toward HL‑LHC.

Open analysis & reproducibility

Workflow design and analysis preservation (Snakemake, containers) for robust, repeatable results.
